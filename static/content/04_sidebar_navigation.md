Sidebar Navigation Guide

The Sidebar is the primary navigation hub of OpenWebUI. It gives you instant access to the core features that define your AI workflow. Always visible on the left side of the screen, the Sidebar ensures you can move between sections with minimal effort.

The Chats section allows you to browse existing conversations or start a new one. The Models tab opens a window into all available AI models, providing insights, configuration options, and tools for managing local and remote models. You may also see Extensions, Prompts, Workspaces, or other custom modules depending on your installation.

Visual clarity is central to the sidebar design. Icons and labels are spaced to reduce clutter, while expand/collapse behavior lets you hide lower-priority sections. Users working in compact screen modes will appreciate how the Sidebar adapts gracefully, preserving readability.

Because the Sidebar is always accessible, it forms the backbone of OpenWebUI navigation—helping users intuitively jump between tools, settings, and conversations without losing context.
