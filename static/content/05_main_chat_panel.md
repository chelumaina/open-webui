Main Chat Panel Guide

The Main Chat Panel is the heart of OpenWebUI—where you interact directly with your AI models. It is designed to offer a fluid chat experience with clean typography, message grouping, and easy-to-read formatting. Whether you’re drafting content, debugging code, or asking general questions, the panel ensures focus remains on the conversation.

Your messages and the model’s responses appear as chat bubbles aligned in a thread. The input box supports rich text, markdown formatting, and attachments depending on your setup. When a model is generating a response, the interface displays a smooth typing indicator that keeps you informed.

Above the chat, contextual actions allow you to rename the conversation, save prompts, or switch models mid-dialogue. Power users can also access message-level tools such as copying, exporting, regenerating, or pinning responses.

The Main Chat Panel’s goal is simple: provide a distraction-free, high-quality conversational experience optimized for daily professional use.
