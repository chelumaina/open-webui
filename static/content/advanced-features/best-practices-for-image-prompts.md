# Best Practices for Image Prompts

To maximize the accuracy and usefulness of AI responses, LexLuma recommends specific best practices when using image prompts for legal research.

---

## 🖼️ Key Guidelines

1. **High Resolution:** Ensure scanned documents and images are clear and legible.  
2. **Proper Orientation:** Rotate images to standard upright orientation to avoid parsing errors.  
3. **Annotations:** Highlight critical sections or notes within images for precise extraction.  
4. **Segmentation:** Split large multi-page scans into smaller segments for context-aware analysis.  
5. **Tagging:** Include tags like case number, document type, or jurisdiction to facilitate retrieval.

---

## 🔧 Using Image Prompts Effectively

- Upload images via the **Documents Panel**.  
- Reference image sections in chat queries using descriptors like “highlighted clause on page 2.”  
- Combine with text-based prompts for multi-modal queries.  
- Review AI-extracted content for critical legal points or citations.

---

## 💡 Best Practices Summary

- Clear, annotated, and well-organized images lead to **higher AI accuracy**.  
- Multi-turn queries enhance understanding of complex visual materials.  
- Use consistent file naming conventions for long-term management.  
- Always verify AI-extracted content against original images for critical legal decisions.
