# Chatting with PDF Files

LexLuma allows users to **interact directly with PDF documents** using the AI chat interface. This feature combines RAG with document parsing, enabling efficient and precise legal research.

---

## 📄 Key Capabilities

- **Search Within PDF:** Extract relevant paragraphs, clauses, or rulings.  
- **Summarization:** Generate concise summaries of lengthy court decisions or gazettes.  
- **Question-Answering:** Ask targeted questions about specific sections.  
- **Cross-Reference:** Compare content across multiple PDFs simultaneously.

---

## 🔧 How to Chat with PDFs

1. Upload the PDF via the **Documents Panel**.  
2. Select the document when starting a new chat.  
3. Ask your questions directly in the chat box.  
4. LexLuma will return answers referencing **page numbers and sections**.  
5. Use **follow-up questions** for multi-step legal analysis.

---

## 💡 Best Practices

- Ensure PDFs are **text-based or OCR-processed** for accurate parsing.  
- Use **keywords** to target specific legal issues.  
- For long PDFs, consider splitting into sections for clarity.  
- Cross-check AI references against the original PDF for critical legal tasks.
