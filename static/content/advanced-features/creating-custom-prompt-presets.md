# Creating Custom Prompt Presets

LexLuma allows users to **create custom prompts** tailored to specific legal workflows. This flexibility ensures AI responses align with your unique research or drafting requirements.

---

## 🛠️ Steps to Create Custom Prompts

1. Open the **Prompt Library** and click **Create New Prompt**.  
2. Enter a **name** and **description** for the preset.  
3. Define instructions clearly, specifying the **desired format, tone, and context**.  
4. Optionally, link the prompt to **specific document types or categories**.  
5. Save and test the prompt in a chat session to verify output accuracy.

---

## 🔑 Key Benefits

- Tailor AI instructions to niche areas of law or specialized research.  
- Maintain workflow consistency across your team or firm.  
- Reuse prompts for repetitive tasks such as drafting opinions, summarizing rulings, or extracting citations.

---

## 💡 Best Practices

- Start with a simple template and refine based on AI responses.  
- Include explicit instructions for legal citation, format, or jurisdiction.  
- Periodically review and update prompts to reflect changes in law or workflow.  
- Share effective prompts within your workspace for collaborative efficiency.
