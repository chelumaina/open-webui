# Document Chat & RAG Overview

LexLuma's **Document Chat** and **RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)** features enable legal professionals to interact directly with their legal documents, statutes, gazettes, and court decisions in a dynamic and context-aware manner. These tools bring a new level of precision and efficiency to research workflows.

---

## 🔍 What is Document Chat?

Document Chat allows users to **query documents directly**, asking specific questions about legal texts. The AI interprets the content, retrieves relevant sections, and provides detailed responses. This feature reduces time spent manually searching statutes or rulings.

### Key Benefits:
- **Contextual Answers:** Responses reference exact paragraphs, clauses, or rulings.  
- **Multi-Document Support:** Query multiple documents simultaneously.  
- **Interactive Workflow:** Ask follow-up questions without losing context.  

---

## 📚 Understanding RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)

RAG enhances LexLuma’s AI by integrating a **knowledge retrieval layer**. When a user asks a question:
1. The system retrieves the most relevant documents from your **legal knowledge base**.  
2. The AI generates responses using both retrieved documents and its trained model knowledge.  

### Advantages:
- Accurate referencing of **gazettes, statutes, or case law**.  
- Faster response generation with **relevant citations**.  
- Reduces hallucinations by grounding answers in actual legal materials.

---

## 💡 Best Practices

- Clearly specify the **document or section** you are querying.  
- Use **multi-turn queries** to explore complex legal questions.  
- Verify RAG-generated citations against the source for critical decisions.  
- Combine with **Document Uploading** and **Chat History** features for organized research.
