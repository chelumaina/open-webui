# Image and Vision File Support

LexLuma provides **vision capabilities** to analyze and interpret images and scanned legal documents. This feature is essential for working with visual exhibits, scanned gazettes, or annotated legal forms.

---

## 🖼️ Supported File Types

- JPEG / PNG / TIFF for scanned evidence or annotated files  
- PDF images with embedded OCR for court filings or gazettes  
- Multi-page image documents for evidence review

---

## 🔍 Key Capabilities

- **OCR (Optical Character Recognition):** Convert scanned images into searchable text.  
- **Image Analysis:** Extract tables, figures, and annotations from visual documents.  
- **Multi-Modal Chat:** Combine vision and text-based AI to answer queries referencing both image and text data.  
- **Cross-Referencing:** Use images alongside text or PDF documents for comprehensive legal research.

---

## 💡 Best Practices

- Ensure images are **high-resolution** to improve OCR accuracy.  
- Annotate images where necessary to highlight legal clauses or sections.  
- Combine **vision models with text-only models** to enhance understanding of complex documents.  
- Regularly verify AI-extracted text against the original image for legal precision.
