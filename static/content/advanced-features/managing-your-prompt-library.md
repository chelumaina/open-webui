# Managing Your Prompt Library

LexLuma allows users to **organize, edit, and maintain** their prompt library for optimal workflow efficiency.

---

## 🗂️ Key Capabilities

- **Edit Prompts:** Modify instructions to reflect updates in law or workflow changes.  
- **Delete Outdated Prompts:** Remove irrelevant prompts to maintain clarity.  
- **Categorize Prompts:** Organize by task type, jurisdiction, or document type.  
- **Search & Filter:** Quickly locate prompts using keywords or tags.  

---

## 🔧 How to Manage

1. Open **Prompt Library** in the sidebar.  
2. Use **search, filter, or category options** to locate prompts.  
3. Edit, duplicate, or delete as necessary.  
4. Save changes and test prompts to ensure expected output.  
5. Pin frequently used prompts for quick access in your workspace.

---

## 💡 Best Practices

- Maintain separate categories for **internal workflows and client-facing tasks**.  
- Keep prompt descriptions clear to avoid confusion among team members.  
- Test new prompts on sample documents before full deployment.  
- Regularly archive prompts to prevent clutter while retaining historical reference.
