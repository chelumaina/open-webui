# Multi-modal Conversations

LexLuma supports **multi-modal conversations**, enabling users to interact with both text and visual documents simultaneously. This advanced feature enhances legal research by integrating RAG, document chat, and vision AI capabilities.

---

## 🔑 Key Capabilities

- Combine **text documents, PDFs, and images** in a single chat session.  
- AI cross-references **visual and textual data** for contextual answers.  
- Supports **multi-turn queries** while maintaining context across document types.  
- Ideal for reviewing exhibits, legal memos, statutes, and case rulings in tandem.

---

## 🔧 How to Use

1. Upload relevant text documents, PDFs, and images into your workspace.  
2. Start a new chat and select multiple documents as context.  
3. Ask questions referencing both text and images, e.g., “Summarize clauses highlighted in the attached PDF exhibit.”  
4. AI provides integrated responses with citations and references to visual content.  
5. Use follow-up queries to refine or expand legal analysis.

---

## 💡 Best Practices

- Clearly label and tag all documents and images for easier context handling.  
- Break down large files into smaller sections for more precise AI responses.  
- Verify AI-generated summaries and citations against original documents.  
- Leverage **workspace pinning** to maintain focus on relevant multi-modal materials.
