# Prompt Presets Introduction

LexLuma offers **Prompt Presets**, pre-defined instructions for the AI to generate consistent, high-quality legal outputs. These presets save time, ensure accuracy, and provide structure for common legal research tasks.

---

## 🔑 Key Benefits

- **Consistency:** Standardize AI responses across multiple users or teams.  
- **Efficiency:** Quickly generate briefs, summaries, or case analyses without typing detailed instructions.  
- **Customizability:** Modify existing prompts or create your own for specialized legal workflows.  
- **Knowledge Integration:** Combine prompts with RAG-based document search for precise, citation-backed results.

---

## 🛠️ How Prompt Presets Work

1. Access the **Prompt Library** in the LexLuma sidebar.  
2. Select a preset relevant to your task (e.g., case summarization, legislative interpretation).  
3. Start a chat, and the AI will automatically follow the instructions in the preset.  
4. You can edit the preset before use for additional context or specificity.

---

## 💡 Best Practices

- Use standard prompts for routine tasks like **statute summarization** or **brief drafting**.  
- Customize prompts for specific jurisdictions or specialized areas of law.  
- Combine with **Document Chat** to enrich responses with citations and extracted content.  
- Periodically review and update presets to reflect changes in law or workflow improvements.
