# Text-to-Speech Output

LexLuma’s **text-to-speech (TTS)** feature allows AI-generated responses to be read aloud. This is useful for reviewing legal analysis, summaries, or multi-page document responses hands-free.

---

## 🔊 Key Capabilities

- **Audio Playback of Responses:** Hear summaries, answers, and document explanations.  
- **Multi-Language Support:** Listen to responses in preferred languages.  
- **Customizable Voice:** Adjust pitch, speed, and tone for comfortable listening.  
- **Accessibility:** Supports users with reading difficulties or those multitasking.

---

## 🛠️ How to Use

1. Click the **Speaker Icon** next to any AI response.  
2. Adjust voice settings as needed in the **Voice Settings Panel**.  
3. Listen to responses in real-time while continuing your research.  
4. Pause or replay audio as required.

---

## 💡 Best Practices

- Use TTS for **document summaries** to save time reading lengthy rulings.  
- Verify that AI-generated speech accurately reflects citations or statutes.  
- Combine with **speech-to-text input** for fully voice-driven research sessions.  
- Use headphones in shared environments for clarity and privacy.
