# Understanding Search Results

LexLuma integrates web search results with your internal knowledge base using RAG, providing concise, accurate, and context-aware responses. Understanding how to interpret these results ensures reliable legal research.

---

## 🔍 Components of Search Results

1. **Summarized Response:**  
   AI presents the key information from retrieved documents or web sources.  

2. **Source References:**  
   Each result includes a citation with a clickable link to the original website or document.  

3. **Relevance Score:**  
   Some results include a relevance indicator, showing how closely the content matches your query.  

4. **Contextual Notes:**  
   AI may highlight related legal clauses, statutes, or precedents.

---

## 🛠️ Using Search Results Effectively

- Verify citations against original sources for **legal accuracy**.  
- Cross-reference with your **internal document library** to validate findings.  
- Use relevance scores and summaries to prioritize the most pertinent legal material.  
- Save or pin important results in your workspace for repeated access.

---

## 💡 Best Practices

- Be specific in queries by including jurisdiction, case numbers, or statutory references.  
- Combine search results with **Document Chat** to enhance context-based analysis.  
- Use follow-up queries to explore alternative interpretations or related cases.  
- Maintain a log of external references for auditing and compliance purposes.
