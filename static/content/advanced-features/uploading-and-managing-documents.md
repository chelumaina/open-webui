# Uploading and Managing Documents

LexLuma allows legal professionals to **upload, manage, and organize documents** directly within the platform. This feature ensures your research materials are accessible, searchable, and ready for Document Chat queries.

---

## 🗂️ Uploading Documents

Supported formats include:
- **PDFs:** Court decisions, statutes, gazettes  
- **Word/Docx:** Legal memos, contracts, reports  
- **Text Files:** Notes, excerpts, briefs  

### Steps to Upload:
1. Navigate to the **Documents Panel** in LexLuma.  
2. Click **Upload** and select your file(s).  
3. Assign a **document name, category, and tags** for easier retrieval.  
4. Wait for indexing; documents are now searchable via RAG.

---

## 🔧 Managing Documents

- **Edit Metadata:** Update names, categories, or tags.  
- **Version Control:** Track revisions for important statutes or rulings.  
- **Delete/Archive:** Remove outdated documents to maintain efficiency.  
- **Search & Filter:** Quickly locate documents by keyword, tag, or category.

---

## 💡 Best Practices

- Use **consistent naming conventions** for easier navigation.  
- Tag documents by type (e.g., case law, legislation, memorandum).  
- Archive outdated versions instead of deleting to maintain historical reference.  
- Periodically review your document library to ensure completeness.
