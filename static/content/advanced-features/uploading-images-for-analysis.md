# Uploading Images for Analysis

LexLuma allows users to upload images and scanned documents for AI-assisted analysis. Proper upload ensures accurate extraction of text and legal insights.

---

## 🛠️ Supported File Types

- JPEG, PNG, TIFF for exhibits or scanned legal documents  
- Multi-page PDFs containing images or scanned documents  
- Annotated diagrams and tables for case or contract analysis

---

## 🔧 Steps to Upload

1. Navigate to **Documents Panel > Upload**.  
2. Select image files from your device.  
3. Optionally, add **tags, categories, or case references** for easy retrieval.  
4. Wait for the AI to index the images and enable chat-based queries.  
5. Access the images from **sidebar > Documents** or directly in chat.

---

## 💡 Best Practices

- Ensure images are **clear and legible** to optimize OCR performance.  
- Tag images by **case, document type, or exhibit number**.  
- Use high-contrast scans for accurate text extraction.  
- Combine with **multi-modal conversations** for integrated text and image analysis.
