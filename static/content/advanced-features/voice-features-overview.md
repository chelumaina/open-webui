# Voice Features Overview

LexLuma provides **voice capabilities** to facilitate speech-to-text input and text-to-speech output, streamlining legal research and communication. Voice features allow legal professionals to work hands-free or process long documents more efficiently.

---

## 🎤 Key Capabilities

- **Voice Input (Speech-to-Text):** Convert spoken questions or notes into AI-interpretable text.  
- **Text-to-Speech Output:** AI can read responses aloud for easy review of legal documents or summaries.  
- **Multi-Language Support:** Supports multiple languages for diverse legal environments.  
- **Customizable Voice Settings:** Adjust pitch, speed, and voice style to suit user preference.

---

## 🛠️ Applications in Legal Work

- Dictate questions while reviewing case files or statutes.  
- Listen to AI-generated summaries or opinions without reading long texts.  
- Enable accessibility for users who prefer audio-based workflows.  
- Combine with Document Chat to query legal materials using voice commands.

---

## 💡 Best Practices

- Use **clear pronunciation** to improve transcription accuracy.  
- Break long queries into shorter sentences for precise understanding.  
- Verify AI-generated voice output for correctness, especially in legal citations.  
- Regularly update voice model preferences for optimal clarity.
