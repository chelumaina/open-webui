# Voice Input (Speech-to-Text)

LexLuma’s **speech-to-text feature** enables users to convert spoken words into text for direct interaction with AI. This is ideal for legal professionals who prefer dictation over typing.

---

## 🎤 How It Works

1. Click the **Microphone Icon** in the chat interface.  
2. Speak clearly into your device’s microphone.  
3. LexLuma transcribes your speech in real-time.  
4. The transcribed text appears in the chat box for further AI processing.

---

## 🛠️ Supported Scenarios

- Dictating **questions about cases or statutes**.  
- Drafting **legal memos or briefs** using voice input.  
- Interactive **voice-assisted research** in multi-turn chats.  

---

## 💡 Best Practices

- Ensure your microphone is of **good quality** and properly configured.  
- Speak at a **moderate pace** for accurate transcription.  
- Avoid background noise to reduce errors.  
- Review transcriptions before sending for AI processing, especially for critical legal queries.
