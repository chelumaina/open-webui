# Voice Settings and Preferences

LexLuma allows customization of **voice input and output settings** to optimize the user experience during legal research.

---

## 🔧 Key Settings

- **Microphone Selection:** Choose your preferred input device.  
- **Voice Speed & Pitch:** Adjust output for comfortable listening.  
- **Language & Accent:** Set speech recognition and TTS language.  
- **Noise Reduction:** Filter background noise for accurate transcription.

---

## 🛠️ How to Configure

1. Go to **Settings > Advanced Features > Voice Settings**.  
2. Adjust microphone, voice style, speed, and language preferences.  
3. Save your configuration for all future voice sessions.  
4. Test settings using the **Test Input/Output** button.

---

## 💡 Best Practices

- Regularly check microphone functionality for accurate transcription.  
- Choose TTS voices that are clear and professional for legal content.  
- Adjust settings based on your working environment (office, courtroom, home).  
- Combine voice settings with document chat for hands-free, efficient workflows.
