# Web Search Integration

LexLuma offers **web search capabilities**, allowing users to query external legal resources alongside your knowledge base. This ensures access to the most current legal information and precedents.

---

## 🔍 Key Features

- **Real-Time Search:** Fetch up-to-date legal articles, gazettes, or rulings.  
- **RAG Integration:** Combine web search results with your internal knowledge base.  
- **Filtered Results:** Narrow searches by jurisdiction, source, or document type.  
- **Citations:** AI provides references to web sources to validate information.

---

## 🔧 How It Works

1. Enable **Web Search** in your LexLuma settings.  
2. Enter queries in the chat interface.  
3. LexLuma retrieves the most relevant results from trusted legal websites.  
4. Results are summarized and integrated into the chat response.  
5. Follow citations to verify the source if needed.

---

## 💡 Best Practices

- Use **specific legal keywords** to improve search relevance.  
- Cross-check AI summaries with original sources for legal accuracy.  
- Combine web search with **document chat** for hybrid legal research.  
- Save frequently used sources to your **workspace** for faster access.
