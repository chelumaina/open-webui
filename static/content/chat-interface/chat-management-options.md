# Chat Management Options

LexLuma offers a comprehensive set of **chat management tools** to keep your legal research organized, accessible, and efficient. Proper management ensures that important conversations remain easy to find, secure, and contextually relevant.

---

## 🛠️ Available Chat Management Tools

### **1. Archive Chats**
Move old or inactive conversations to the archive to declutter your sidebar while retaining full access when needed.

### **2. Delete Chats**
Remove irrelevant or outdated conversations permanently. Use this carefully to avoid losing valuable research.

### **3. Pin Chats**
Pin high-priority or frequently used chats to the top of the sidebar for quick access.

### **4. Search & Filter**
Quickly locate conversations based on keywords, date ranges, or participants. See also: [/sidebar-navigation/searching-and-filtering-chats](#)

### **5. Rename Chats**
Assign meaningful names reflecting cases, statutes, or matters. See also: [/chat-interface/renaming-and-organizing-chats](#)

---

## 📂 Organizing for Legal Work

Proper chat organization is crucial when handling multiple cases, jurisdictions, or projects simultaneously:

- **Case-based folders:** Group chats by case number or client.  
- **Topic-based folders:** Separate constitutional, corporate, or criminal law matters.  
- **Time-based archiving:** Move resolved matters to reduce clutter.  

This system enhances efficiency for lawyers, clerks, and researchers navigating multiple matters.

---

## 🔐 Security and Compliance

- Archived chats remain accessible only to authorized users.  
- LexLuma maintains a secure audit trail for all actions, ensuring regulatory compliance.  
- Sensitive case material can be encrypted or restricted to specific workspaces.
