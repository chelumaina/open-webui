# Clearing Conversation History

Clearing conversation history in LexLuma is a powerful tool to maintain privacy, reduce clutter, or reset context for a new legal matter. It ensures that your AI interactions remain focused and organized.

---

## 🧹 How to Clear History

1. Open the conversation you wish to clear.  
2. Click the **Chat Management Options** menu.  
3. Select **Clear Conversation History**.  
4. Confirm the action.  

All messages and AI context within that conversation will be removed. Historical backups may still exist depending on workspace settings.

---

## 🔍 Why Clear History?

- **Protect confidential information** when a matter concludes  
- **Reset AI context** for a new research task  
- **Reduce sidebar clutter** for active workspaces  
- **Comply with data retention policies**

---

## ⚠️ Important Notes

- Clearing history is **irreversible** within the chat interface.  
- Make sure to **export important content** before clearing.  
- Only users with proper permissions can clear workspace-shared conversations.  

---

## 🧠 Best Practices

- Archive completed matters before clearing.  
- Use consistent naming and workspace structure to avoid accidental deletions.  
- Consider clearing **session-level context** rather than the entire conversation when only resetting AI understanding.
