# Copying and Sharing Messages

LexLuma makes it easy to copy or share individual messages from your conversations. Whether you are preparing a brief, emailing a colleague, or inserting a summary into your legal management system, copying messages helps streamline your workflow.

---

## 📋 Copying Messages

To copy any message:
1. Hover over the message.  
2. Click the **Copy** icon in the Message Actions Menu.  
3. The text is copied to your clipboard instantly.  

Copied messages retain formatting including:
- Bullet points  
- Citations  
- Headings  
- Numbered lists  

This ensures clean pasting into Word, email, or internal legal tools.

---

## 📤 Sharing Messages

Share options allow you to:
- Export a message as text  
- Generate a shareable snippet  
- Transfer content into another workspace  
- Forward part of a conversation to a team member  

Depending on your LexLuma plan, links may be:

- **Private:** accessible only within your organization  
- **Public (temporary):** shareable externally for collaboration  

---

## 💡 Use Cases for Legal Professionals

### **1. Collaborative Drafting**
Share AI-generated paragraphs with fellow lawyers.

### **2. Snippet Extraction**
Quickly copy key holdings, arguments, or statutory extracts.

### **3. Internal Research Notes**
Send excerpts to clerks, interns, or research assistants.

### **4. Presentations or Reports**
Copy formatted summaries directly into reports or legislative briefs.

---

## 🧠 Tips for Effective Use

- Use **Copy** for clean text transfer.  
- Use **Share** when multiple team members need real-time collaboration.  
- When sharing externally, confirm that confidential information is removed.
