# Exporting Conversations

LexLuma allows you to **export conversations** for legal documentation, collaboration, or filing purposes. This ensures that AI-generated insights are preserved, shareable, and usable in formal legal workflows.

---

## 📤 Export Options

1. **PDF**
   - Ideal for court submissions, memos, or briefs  
   - Maintains formatting, headings, bullet points, and citations

2. **Word Document (.docx)**
   - Editable format for drafting and editing within your firm’s workflow  

3. **Plain Text**
   - Useful for internal notes, copying into research databases, or scripting purposes  

4. **CSV**
   - For extracting structured information such as issue lists, summaries, or case references  

---

## 🛠️ How to Export

1. Hover over the conversation or access the **chat management menu**.  
2. Select **Export Conversation**.  
3. Choose the desired file type and location.  
4. Download or share with authorized colleagues.

---

## 🔐 Considerations

- Ensure **client confidentiality** before exporting.  
- Check for **RAG citation visibility**—sources should remain intact.  
- Maintain proper **version control** if exporting multiple drafts of research.  

---

## 📘 Tips for Legal Professionals

- Export case summaries for filing or review.  
- Use Word exports for drafting pleadings and internal notes.  
- Combine exports with chat renaming for easy future retrieval.  
- Regularly archive exported conversations for compliance and auditing purposes.
