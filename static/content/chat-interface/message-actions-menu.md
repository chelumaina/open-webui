# Message Actions Menu

Every message in LexLuma includes a **Message Actions Menu**, giving you precise control over how you interact with your conversation. These tools streamline research workflows, allow fine-tuning of responses, and help you maintain a clean and organized chat environment.

---

## 🎛️ How to Access the Menu

Hover over or tap any message to reveal a small action toolbar. Select the **⋮ More Actions** icon to open the full menu.

---

## 🧰 Available Message Actions

### **1. Copy Message**
Copies the full content to your clipboard—useful when pasting excerpts into briefs, submissions, or legal notes.

### **2. Regenerate Response**
Asks LexLuma to produce a new interpretation or variation of the previous answer. Ideal when:
- You want a different writing tone  
- Additional details are needed  
- You want the model to reconsider reasoning  

See more: [/chat-interface/regenerating-responses](#)

### **3. Edit Your Message**
Allows you to correct typos, adjust a question, or refine your prompt without starting a new chat.

### **4. Delete Message**
Removes your message or an AI response from the conversation timeline. History remains otherwise unaffected.

### **5. Cite Sources**
For responses generated using RAG, you can view or expand the legal citations used to support the answer.

### **6. Expand or Collapse**
Useful for long answers such as case summaries or legal analyses.

### **7. Share or Export**
Copies a sharable snippet or sends the message to your export tool.

---

## ⭐ Best Use Cases

- Refine prompts with “Edit Message” instead of resending.  
- Use “Regenerate” for alternative legal interpretations.  
- Hide long messages using “Collapse” to keep chats tidy.  
- Use “Copy” to easily transfer content into drafting tools.
