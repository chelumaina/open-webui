# Renaming and Organizing Chats

LexLuma allows you to **rename and structure chats** to maintain clarity and quick retrieval. Effective organization is critical for legal professionals who manage multiple cases, statutes, or research topics simultaneously.

---

## ✏️ Renaming Chats

1. Hover over the chat in the sidebar.  
2. Click the **Rename** option.  
3. Enter a descriptive title, e.g., *“Civil Case - Section 45 Review”*.  
4. Press **Enter** to save.

Descriptive titles help identify relevant research quickly and avoid accidental duplicates.

---

## 🗂️ Organizing Chats into Workspaces

Workspaces are a collection of related chats. For example:
- Client-based workspace (*Client ABC Legal Research*)  
- Statute-specific workspace (*Corporate Governance Act Analysis*)  
- Department workspace (*Prosecutor Office – Criminal Matters*)

Features:
- Drag and drop chats into workspaces  
- Assign different team permissions  
- Filter and search within a workspace  

See also: [/sidebar-navigation/creating-and-managing-workspaces](#)

---

## 🔍 Search and Filter

Combine **renaming** and **workspace structuring** with search and filter tools for maximum efficiency:
- Filter by date  
- Filter by participants  
- Keyword search within chats  

This ensures that lawyers and clerks can locate a specific conversation within seconds.

---

## 🧠 Best Practices

- Use **consistent naming conventions** for cases and statutes.  
- Archive completed chats to reduce clutter.  
- Pin active chats for ongoing matters.  
- Keep workspace organization aligned with firm structure for easy access.
