# Basic Layout Overview

LexLuma features a clean, modern interface designed for legal professionals who need fast, structured access to information. Understanding the layout will help you navigate efficiently and keep your research organized.

---

## 🧭 Main Interface Sections

LexLuma is divided into three core areas:

### **1. Left Sidebar – Navigation Hub**

This panel is your central access point for:

- Chats & research threads  
- Uploaded documents  
- Saved notes  
- Workspace folders  

You can collapse or expand the sidebar for a distraction-free view of your workspace.

---

### **2. Center Workspace – Your Main Research Area**

This is where all interaction with the AI happens.

- Type questions directly  
- Upload legal documents (PDF, text, excerpts)  
- Review structured responses  
- Continue context-aware conversations  

LexLuma formats answers with headings, bullet lists, and references, making complex material easier to follow.

---

### **3. Right Panel – Contextual Tools**

Depending on what you’re doing, the right panel may display:

- Extracted citations  
- Related statutes or cases  
- Document summaries  
- Quick actions such as “Compare,” “Summarize,” or “Extract Issues”  

This panel appears when relevant and automatically hides when not needed.

---

## 📍 Top Navigation Bar

The header contains:

- **Profile & Settings**  
- **Light/Dark Mode**  
- **Help & Documentation**  
- **Organization panel (if applicable)**  

From here, you can adjust your experience or reach support resources quickly.
