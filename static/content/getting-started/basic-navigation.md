# Basic Navigation

Navigating LexLuma is designed to feel familiar and efficient. By understanding the core navigation elements, you can move between research threads, documents, and tools without losing momentum.

---

## 🗂️ Sidebar Navigation

The **left sidebar** acts as your primary navigation hub. From here, you can:

- Open existing chats  
- Create new research threads  
- Access uploaded documents  
- Organize work into folders or categories  

Each chat preserves its own context, enabling seamless follow-up queries.

---

## 💬 Chat Thread Navigation

Inside a chat:

- Scroll through previous responses  
- Click timestamps or sections for quick movement  
- Copy excerpts for reference  
- Search within the thread (if available)  

Rename conversations to keep matters organized, such as  
**“Criminal Appeal – Sentencing Issues”** or **“Contract Law – Breach Analysis.”**

---

## 🧰 Top Menu Essentials

The top navigation bar grants access to:

- **Settings**  
- **Help & Documentation**  
- **Profile preferences**  
- Light/dark mode  

These tools help personalize your workspace and troubleshoot when needed.

---

## ⚡ Efficiency Tips

- Use “New Chat” when switching to a different matter.  
- Keep separate threads for distinct issues within the same case.  
- Make use of quick actions in the right panel when analyzing documents.
