# First Login & Account Creation

Setting up your LexLuma account is quick and secure. Whether you are using a personal login or accessing LexLuma through your organization, the process ensures privacy, confidentiality, and smooth onboarding.

---

## 📝 Step 1: Creating Your Account

1. Visit **https://lexluma.com**.  
2. Click **Sign Up** from the top navigation bar.  
3. Fill in the required fields:
   - Full name  
   - Email address  
   - Secure password  
4. If your organization uses **Single Sign-On (SSO)**, choose the appropriate login provider.

Once submitted, LexLuma will send a verification link to your email. Click the link to activate your account.

---

## 🔑 Step 2: Logging In

Return to **lexluma.com** and select **Login**. Enter your credentials or use SSO (if applicable). Should you forget your password, the **Forgot Password** option allows secure resetting.

Upon your first login, you’ll be guided through an onboarding walkthrough highlighting essential interface sections:

- **Sidebar navigation**  
- **Chat workspace**  
- **Document tools**  
- **Settings menu**  

You may also be prompted to select your professional role (e.g., Advocate, Law Clerk, Prosecutor) to personalize interface tips and recommended workflows.

---

## 🔒 Security & Data Protection

LexLuma stores all research queries, uploaded documents, conversations, and drafts securely. Your data is visible only to you unless organizational sharing is enabled by your administrator.

- Encrypted connections  
- Private user history  
- No unauthorized data access
