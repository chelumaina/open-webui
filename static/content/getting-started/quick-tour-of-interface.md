# Quick Tour of the LexLuma Interface

LexLuma’s interface is engineered to support the demanding needs of legal practitioners. This quick tour introduces each major component so you can navigate confidently from day one.

---

## 🚩 1. Sidebar (Left Panel)

This is your control center for:

- Viewing previous chats  
- Organizing research by topic or case  
- Accessing uploaded documents  
- Creating new conversations  

You can easily search, sort, or rename chats to maintain clarity across multiple matters.

---

## 📖 2. Chat Workspace (Center Panel)

The heart of your research workflow.

Here you will:

- Enter questions  
- Upload case files or excerpts  
- Review detailed, structured answers  
- Ask follow-ups and refine requests  

LexLuma presents information using headlines, bullet points, definitions, comparisons, and citations—making complex topics easy to understand.

---

## 📚 3. Context Tools (Right Panel)

This dynamic panel appears whenever useful. It may show:

- Extracted case citations  
- Related statutory references  
- Document summaries  
- “Quick actions” such as **Summarize**, **Compare**, or **Extract Arguments**  

It enhances your workflow without cluttering the main workspace.

---

## ⚙️ 4. Top Menu Bar

Located at the very top, it includes:

- Profile settings  
- Help resources  
- Theme switch (light/dark)  
- Organization information  
- Account preferences  

This bar ensures essential tools are always within reach.
