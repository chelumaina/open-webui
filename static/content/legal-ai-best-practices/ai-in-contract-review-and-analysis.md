# AI in Contract Review and Analysis

AI significantly speeds up contract review by automating the identification of clauses, risks, and deviations from standard templates. Modern AI tools can extract definitions, obligations, indemnities, warranties, termination terms, and key dates with high accuracy.

---
For transactional attorneys, AI can compare incoming contracts against a firm’s clause library and highlight unusual or non-market terms. It is especially powerful in due diligence workflows, where dozens or hundreds of contracts must be analyzed for red flags.

---
RAG-based systems allow attorneys to ask targeted questions, such as:
  - Does this contract contain a non-compete clause?
  - What are the termination rights?
  - Summarize all payment obligations.


---
AI-generated summaries provide quick orientation, but attorneys must still evaluate the legal implications. Customizing AI prompts can also help generate negotiation points, alternative clause suggestions, or clean contract drafts based on prior deals.

---
By combining AI efficiency with attorney review, firms reduce turnaround times while maintaining contractual accuracy and quality.
