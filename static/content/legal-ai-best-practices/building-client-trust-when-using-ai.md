# Building Client Trust When Using AI

Clients increasingly expect law firms to use modern technology, but they also want assurance that AI is used responsibly. Transparency is key. Explain the role AI plays in your workflow—for example, drafting preliminary versions of documents or accelerating research—while emphasizing that attorneys always review outputs.

---
Highlight the benefits clients receive: faster turnaround times, improved accuracy, and lower costs for routine work. Make clear that no confidential information is shared with unsecure services and that the firm uses AI tools that comply with data protection regulations.

---
If clients express concerns, offer to walk them through your safeguards, such as encryption, access controls, and document verification procedures. Provide examples of how AI improved efficiency or preserved accuracy in past matters (without disclosing privileged details).

---
Building trust involves reinforcing that AI is a support mechanism—not a substitute for legal expertise. By communicating openly and demonstrating responsible usage, firms strengthen relationships and position themselves as forward-thinking and client-centered.
