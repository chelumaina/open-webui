# Client Communication Best Practices  
Using AI responsibly includes clear and thoughtful communication with clients. Whether you use LexLuma for drafting, research, or reviewing documents, transparency and professionalism are essential to maintaining client trust.


---

## 🗣 When to Inform Clients  
Depending on your jurisdiction and practice area, you may need to inform clients if:
- AI significantly contributes to deliverables  
- sensitive client data is processed using AI tools  
- AI-assisted analysis influences legal strategy  

Even when not strictly required, voluntary transparency can build trust.


---

## 🧰 How to Explain AI Usage to Clients  
Keep explanations simple, focusing on benefits and safeguards:

### **Example Script**
> “We use a secure AI-powered legal assistant to speed up research and improve document analysis. All information remains confidential, encrypted, and under our full control. Every AI-generated output is reviewed by a licensed attorney.”


---

## 💬 Best Practices for Communicating AI-Assisted Work  

### **1. Emphasize Attorney Oversight**  
Reassure clients that human lawyers remain fully responsible for decisions and legal conclusions.

### **2. Describe Security Measures**  
Explain LexLuma’s encrypted environment, zero-training policy for user data, and secure document processing.

### **3. Manage Expectations**  
Clarify that:
- AI accelerates tasks  
- AI does not make legal decisions  
- all outputs undergo manual review  

### **4. Obtain Necessary Consents**  
In regulated sectors (healthcare, finance, government), informed consent may be required before processing documents.


---

## 📌 Situations Where Communication Is Critical  
- Cross-border matters involving data protection laws  
- Sensitive litigation  
- Class actions and multi-party disputes  
- Government or public-sector advice  
- High-value contract negotiations
