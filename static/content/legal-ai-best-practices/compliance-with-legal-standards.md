# Compliance with Legal Standards  
Using AI in legal practice requires strict adherence to regulatory frameworks, ethical codes, and professional guidelines. Although LexLuma provides secure, compliant infrastructure, attorneys must still ensure their own usage aligns with jurisdictional rules.


---

## ⚖️ Key Areas of Compliance

### **1. Professional Conduct Rules**  
Most jurisdictions enforce principles requiring lawyers to maintain competence, confidentiality, and independent judgment. AI must be used in a way that complements—not replaces—these obligations.

### **2. Client Confidentiality Laws**  
Rules governing privilege and confidentiality vary by jurisdiction. AI usage must never compromise attorney-client privilege or expose sensitive materials.

### **3. Data Protection Laws**  
Depending on location and client profile, compliance may include:  
- GDPR  
- HIPAA (for health-related matters)  
- UK Data Protection Act  
- CCPA  
Always verify which standards apply to your practice.

### **4. AI-Specific Regulations**  
AI governance frameworks increasingly address:  
- transparency  
- documentation  
- bias prevention  
- accountability  
Legal practitioners should monitor evolving guidelines from bar associations or regulatory bodies.

---

## 📋 Compliance Checklist  
- Are you using AI only for tasks permitted by regulations?  
- Are you informing clients when required?  
- Are outputs verified and documented?  
- Are confidential documents shared appropriately?  
- Are you aware of your jurisdiction’s AI ethics rules?
