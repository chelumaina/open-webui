# Documenting AI Usage  
Proper documentation of AI-assisted work ensures accountability, transparency, and compliance with legal and ethical standards. For legal practitioners, tracking how LexLuma assists with research, drafting, and analysis is essential—especially when AI-generated content influences case strategy or client deliverables.


---

## 📑 Why Documentation Matters  
Thorough documentation helps legal teams:
- Maintain a clear audit trail.  
- Demonstrate due diligence.  
- Meet ethical disclosure requirements (when applicable).  
- Support internal quality assurance processes.  
- Provide evidence of responsible use if issues arise.

AI is a productivity tool, but human oversight remains the core of legal practice. Documenting AI's role reinforces that distinction.


---

## 🧰 What to Document

### **1. Purpose of AI Assistance**  
Record why you used AI in a particular task:
- Drafting a clause  
- Summarizing a deposition  
- Identifying relevant precedents  

### **2. Specific Prompts or Instructions**  
Store the exact prompts used to generate AI outputs. This helps replicate or review decisions later.

### **3. AI Outputs**  
Archive the generated content, such as:
- RAG-sourced citations  
- Summaries  
- Analysis drafts  
- Contract rewrite suggestions  

### **4. Human Review Notes**  
Add annotations explaining adjustments or corrections you made.

### **5. Final Decisions**  
Clarify how the AI-assisted analysis influenced the final document or strategy.


---

## 🗂 Recommended Storage Practices  
- Use matter-based folders in your document management system.  
- Attach AI-generated materials to case notes or research files.  
- Do not store sensitive prompts in unsecured locations.  
- Use LexLuma’s built-in export features for long-form chats.


---

## 📌 Documentation Templates  
**Example Entry:**  
- **Task:** Summarize 2023 contract amendments  
- **AI Tool:** LexLuma RAG Model  
- **Prompt:** “Summarize the key changes in Section 4 and highlight risk areas.”  
- **AI Output:** Generated summary + citations  
- **Human Review:** Confirmed accuracy; added client-specific risk notes  
- **Outcome:** Incorporated summary into client memo
