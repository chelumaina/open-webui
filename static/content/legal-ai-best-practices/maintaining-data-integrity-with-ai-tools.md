# Maintaining Data Integrity with AI Tools

Maintaining data integrity is critical when incorporating AI tools into legal workflows. Because the accuracy of AI outputs depends on the quality of inputs, firms must establish controls for how documents are stored, labeled, and processed.

---
Begin with clear naming conventions and document categorization. AI performs better when files are organized by matter number, document type, jurisdiction, or client category. Ensure scanned documents are OCR-processed to avoid errors in text extraction.

---
AI platforms should be integrated with trusted, secure document repositories to ensure only the most current versions are used. Version control is essential—using outdated drafts can lead to incorrect conclusions or irrelevant analyses.

---
Firms must also implement validation checkpoints. Before AI-generated summaries or analyses are used in filings, attorneys should cross-check citations, ensure dates and names are accurate, and confirm no key facts are missing.

---
Maintaining data integrity enhances the reliability of AI outputs and protects against inadvertent legal risk.
