# Quick Reference for Legal AI Features

This quick reference guide provides an at-a-glance overview of essential AI capabilities commonly used in legal practice. These tools streamline research, drafting, client communication, and internal workflows while improving accuracy and efficiency.

## Document Chat & RAG:
Enables targeted queries within uploaded documents, extracting relevant sections, summarizing content, and identifying clauses. Ideal for contract review, discovery management, and statutory analysis.

## Legal Drafting Tools:
Generate first drafts of contracts, memos, motions, and client correspondence. Can follow firm-specific templates, styles, and tone guidelines.

## Case Law Search & Analysis:
Provides concise case summaries, identifies relevant precedents, and compares fact patterns. Useful for quick orientation before deeper research.

## Voice Dictation & Transcription:
Supports hands-free drafting, meeting note capture, and deposition summarization with speaker identification.

## Vision & OCR Features:
Extract text from PDFs, scanned contracts, handwritten notes, and evidence photos. Useful for processing legacy documents.

## AI Writing Review:
Performs proofreading, clarity improvements, risk-flagging, and citation checks to assist in polishing drafts.

This reference enables legal professionals to quickly determine which AI feature fits a given task and how to integrate it effectively into legal workflows.
