Top Navigation Bar Guide

The Top Navigation Bar in OpenWebUI provides quick access to essential controls and contextual actions. It is designed to stay lightweight yet powerful, offering everything you need without overwhelming the screen. This bar changes dynamically depending on the page you’re viewing, ensuring that only relevant tools appear at any given moment.

The centerpiece of the navigation bar is the Model Selector, which allows you to switch instantly between available AI models. This enables seamless transitions from lightweight chat models to more advanced reasoning systems without leaving your current workflow. Alongside the model selector, you will find quick-action buttons such as clearing the chat, regenerating responses, or accessing conversation summaries.

Depending on your configuration, the top bar may also include profile settings, notifications, or workspace indicators. For users managing multiple conversations or queues, the navigation bar acts as a stable anchor for high-frequency actions.

Overall, the Top Navigation Bar is built for speed, convenience, and clarity—making your interaction with OpenWebUI efficient and intuitive.
