Right Panel Guide

The Right Panel serves as an auxiliary workspace that enhances your productivity in OpenWebUI. While optional depending on your theme or configuration, this panel can display tools, conversation metadata, plugin results, search results, or additional model outputs. It operates as a context-aware sidebar, adapting its content to the active task.

For example, when working with documents, the panel may show summaries or extracted data. When using prompts or system instructions, it may display templates or presets. Developers may use it for logs or API insights during testing.

Its collapsible design allows you to expand the workspace for tasks that require focus or reopen it when supportive tools are needed. The Right Panel is ideal for power users who rely on enhanced utilities without interrupting the flow of the main chat.

Ultimately, this panel acts as an extension of your workflow—offering reference material, advanced features, or multi-view capabilities within the same screen.
