Navigation Tips & Best Practices

Mastering OpenWebUI’s navigation can dramatically improve speed and productivity. The interface is intentionally designed for efficiency, with shortcuts and workflows that minimize unnecessary clicks. Below are practical tips to help you get the most out of the platform.

Use the Sidebar for quick switching between chats, models, and extensions.

Pin frequently used conversations to keep them accessible at the top.

Switch models directly from the Top Navigation Bar without opening additional menus.

Collapse the Sidebar when working on narrow screens or when focusing on long-form content.

Use the Right Panel for reference material or summaries instead of opening new tabs.

Rename conversations early to make search and organization easier later.

Use keyboard shortcuts (if enabled) to clear chats, send prompts, or navigate sections faster.

With these tips, you’ll navigate the UI with confidence and significantly improve your workflow efficiency.
