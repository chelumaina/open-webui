# Changing Display Name and Avatar

Your display name and avatar appear in chat threads, exported documents, and team rosters. This page explains how to keep your identity professional and consistent across LexLuma, which matters for authorship and accountability in legal workflows.

## Display Name Best Practices

- Use full name and title (e.g., *Jane Doe, Senior Counsel*) for clarity.  
- Include department or role if you handle multiple practice areas.  
- Keep the name concise for exports and headers.

## Avatar Guidance

- Preferred: professional headshot or firm logo.  
- Size/format: upload square JPG or PNG, minimum 256x256 px.  
- Avoid: low-resolution, blurred, or unprofessional images—these may confuse clients or colleagues.

## How to Change Name & Avatar

1. `Settings → Personal → Profile`  
2. Edit the **Display Name** field and click **Save**.  
3. Click **Change Avatar** to upload a file, crop it if prompted, and confirm.  
4. Optionally set avatar visibility (public, organization-only, private).

## Audit & Compliance

- Changes to display name are recorded in the audit log for traceability.  
- In organizational accounts, admins may restrict display name edits to maintain firm-wide consistency.

## Troubleshooting

- If avatar upload fails, confirm file type and size.  
- For SSO-managed accounts, some fields may be locked—contact your administrator.
