# Chat Notification Preferences

Chat notifications help you stay on top of replies, mentions, or important updates within your research threads without being overwhelmed.

## Options

- **All messages:** Receive a notification for every new message (not recommended for high-volume users).  
- **Mentions only:** Notify when someone mentions you or assigns a task.  
- **Pinned chats only:** Notify for activity in pinned or high-priority matters.  
- **Mute chat:** Silence notifications for a specific chat.

## How to Configure

1. `Open chat → Click chat header → Notifications`  
2. Choose between All / Mentions / Pinned / Mute.  
3. For global defaults: `Settings → Notifications → Chat`.

## Best Practices for Legal Workflows

- Use **Mentions only** for collaborative research to avoid notification fatigue.  
- Pin priority cases and enable notifications only for those threads.  
- During court or hearings, set global status to **Do Not Disturb**.
