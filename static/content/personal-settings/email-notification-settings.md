# Email Notification Settings

Email notifications provide a reliable channel for updates when you’re away from LexLuma. Configure frequency, content and scope to match legal workflows and security needs.

## Frequency Options

- **Immediate:** Sent as events occur (recommended for security-critical alerts).  
- **Hourly digest:** Aggregated updates for active matters.  
- **Daily digest:** End-of-day summaries for inbox-friendly reviews.  
- **Off:** Disable email notifications (not recommended for administrators).

## Content Controls

- **Include chat snippets:** Toggle whether message excerpts appear in email.  
- **Include attachments:** Toggle whether attachments are included or only linked.  
- **Security & admin:** Always deliver via email regardless of other settings.

## How to Set

`Settings → Notifications → Email` — choose frequency and content toggles. Confirm preferred email address under `Account`.

## Best Practices

- Use **Immediate** for security alerts, **Daily** for activity summaries.  
- Disable attachments in email for sensitive materials to reduce exposure.  
- Encourage team members to standardize digest settings for consistent review processes.
