# Notification Settings

LexLuma notifications keep you informed about chat activity, document updates, security alerts, and system changes. Tailor notification preferences to avoid noise while ensuring you don’t miss urgent case developments.

## Notification Categories

- **Chat activity:** Replies to pinned chats, mentions, or assigned tasks.  
- **Document updates:** New uploads, indexing completion, or shared documents.  
- **Security alerts:** Suspicious login, credential changes, MFA issues.  
- **System updates:** Feature releases or scheduled maintenance.  
- **Billing & Admin:** Subscription and organizational notices.

## Where to Configure

`Settings → Notifications` — toggle categories and choose delivery channels.

## Delivery Channels

- **In-app:** Banners and badges within LexLuma.  
- **Email:** Digest or immediate alerts.  
- **Push:** Browser or mobile push for critical items (if enabled).  
- **SMS:** For urgent security alerts (optional).

## Recommended Settings for Legal Users

- Enable **security alerts** on all channels.  
- Set **chat mentions** to immediate in-app or push.  
- Use email digests for daily summaries of research activity.  
- Disable non-essential push notifications during court hearings.
