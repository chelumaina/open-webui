# Profile Management Overview

Your **Profile Management** area is the central hub for personalizing LexLuma to fit how you work. This page explains core profile features, where to find them, and how they relate to security, collaboration, and productivity for legal professionals.

## What Lives in Profile Management

- **Basic identity:** Display name, title, email address and contact details.  
- **Avatar:** Upload a professional photo or logo.  
- **Role & preferences:** Select role (advocate, clerk, prosecutor, legislator) to tailor UX tips.  
- **Visibility & privacy:** Choose what others in your organization can see.  
- **Linked accounts:** SSO or organization directory connections.

## Why This Matters for Legal Users

A well-maintained profile aids collaboration and audit trails. When working in a firm or prosecution office, consistent profile data helps assign authorship to research outputs, track responsibility, and personalize shared templates or prompts. Your role selection also helps LexLuma surface relevant presets and documentation.

## Quick Actions

- Navigate: `Settings → Personal → Profile`  
- Update your display name and title for client-facing exports.  
- Upload or remove an avatar.  
- Link or unlink organization SSO (if applicable).

## Best Practices

- Use a professional headshot or your firm logo for clarity in team contexts.  
- Keep email and contact info current to receive security notifications and billing notices.  
- Set visibility conservatively when working with confidential matters.
