# Theme Selection (Light / Dark / Auto)

Theme selection affects UI contrast, color balance, and the overall feel of LexLuma. Choose a theme that fits your workflow and environment.

## Options

- **Light:** Standard bright UI suited for well-lit offices and printed previews.  
- **Dark:** Low-light friendly; reduces eye strain during extended sessions.  
- **Auto:** Matches your OS or browser theme.

## How to Switch

1. `Settings → Appearance → Theme`  
2. Select Light, Dark, or Auto.  
3. Optionally enable **Schedule Mode** to auto-switch at specific times.

## Considerations for Legal Professionals

- Use **Light** when preparing documents for print or clients who prefer traditional layouts.  
- Use **Dark** for drafting at night or reviewing long judgments.  
- Auto mode is convenient when switching between devices with different defaults.

## Accessibility

- High-contrast color options are available in `Settings → Accessibility` for users who need stronger visual differentiation.
