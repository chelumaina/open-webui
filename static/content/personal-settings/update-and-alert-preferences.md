# Update and Alert Preferences

Manage how LexLuma informs you about product updates, policy changes, or urgent alerts. Proper configuration helps legal teams stay compliant while avoiding unnecessary interruptions.

## Types of Alerts

- **Release notes:** Feature updates and changelogs.  
- **Policy changes:** Privacy, terms, or RAG source updates.  
- **Urgent alerts:** Security incidents or downtime.  
- **Legal/regulatory updates:** Notifications about jurisdictional changes when integrated.

## How to Configure

`Settings → Notifications → Updates & Alerts` — choose preferred channels and opt-ins for legal/regulatory notifications.

## Best Practices

- Opt into **Release notes** for admin users to track changes impacting workflows.  
- Ensure **Urgent alerts** come through multiple channels (in-app + email).  
- Centralize policy-change notifications to compliance officers or administrators.
