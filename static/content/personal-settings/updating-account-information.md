# Updating Account Information

Keeping your account information current is essential for security, billing, notifications, and collaboration. This guide walks you through the fields you can change, how to do it, and precautions for legal professionals handling sensitive client matters.

## Editable Fields

- **Primary email address:** For login, password resets, and notifications.  
- **Secondary email / recovery:** For account recovery and alerts.  
- **Phone number:** Optional — used for 2FA and critical alerts.  
- **Organization & department:** Helps group access and workspace mapping.  
- **Time zone & locale:** Ensures timestamps and regional formats align with your practice.

## How to Update

1. Go to `Settings → Personal → Account`.  
2. Edit fields and click **Save**. Some changes (email) will trigger a verification flow.  
3. For organizational profile changes, contact your admin if SSO is enforced.

## Security Notes

- Changing your primary email will send a confirmation link to both old and new addresses.  
- Updates to recovery info should be done from a secure network.  
- If you detect suspicious changes, contact support immediately.

## Practical Tips for Legal Users

- Use a firm-controlled email for workspace consistency.  
- Avoid personal-only email addresses where client-sensitive workflows are used.  
- Keep time zone correct to avoid confusion in court filing deadlines and timestamps.
