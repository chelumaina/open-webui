# Chats Section Overview

The **Chats Section** in the LexLuma sidebar serves as your central hub for accessing, reviewing, and organizing all your research conversations. Since every legal matter often involves multiple issues, documents, and iterations of research, the Chats Section ensures your workflow remains structured and intuitive.

---

## 🧭 What the Chats Section Contains

When you open LexLuma, the sidebar automatically displays your recent and active chats. Each entry represents a full conversation thread, including:

- Your questions  
- AI responses  
- Uploaded documents  
- Follow-up queries  
- Extracted citations and references  

This creates a chronological record of your legal research, similar to maintaining a case file or digital brief.

---

## 📂 Key Functional Areas

### **1. Recent Chats**
Displays your latest conversations for quick access. You can instantly return to ongoing work without searching for it manually.

### **2. Folder or Workspace View**
If you use **Workspaces** to group matters (e.g., “Land Court Case,” “Employment Dispute,” “Legislative Drafting”), the Chats Section reflects the folder structure.

### **3. Tools Menu**
Located beside each chat:
- Rename chat  
- Move to workspace  
- Pin or unpin for priority  
- Delete or archive  

This lets you maintain professional order across parallel matters.

---

## 📌 Why This Matters for Legal Professionals

Legal tasks often involve:
- Revisiting previous authorities  
- Tracking reasoning  
- Managing multiple active cases  

The Chats Section functions like a digital research archive, enabling effortless switching between matters without losing context.
