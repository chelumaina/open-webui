# Creating and Managing Workspaces

Creating Workspaces in LexLuma allows you to structure your legal research with precision. Whether you’re tracking a complex litigation matter or conducting comparative legislative research, Workspaces ensure everything stays organized and accessible.

---

## 🏗️ Creating a New Workspace

In the sidebar, select **➕ New Workspace**.  
You’ll be prompted to:

- Name the workspace  
- Choose an optional description  
- Select visibility (personal or organizational if available)

Create workspaces using meaningful titles such as:

- *“Land Court – Petition 112/2025”*  
- *“Commercial Contracts Review”*  
- *“Legislative Research – Amendments 2024”*

---

## 📂 Adding and Moving Chats

Once your workspace is created, you can:

- Move existing chats into it  
- Start new chats directly within the workspace  
- Group files and notes under the same structure  

This ensures all research for a matter remains consolidated.

---

## ✏️ Editing Workspaces

Workspaces can be updated anytime:

- Rename workspace  
- Change visibility  
- Delete or archive  
- Add descriptive tags  

These tools help maintain a professional research environment.

---

## 🧹 Managing Workspace Clutter

Large legal projects generate many threads. Use workspace:

- Archiving  
- Sorting  
- Filters  

to keep long-term research neat and navigable.
