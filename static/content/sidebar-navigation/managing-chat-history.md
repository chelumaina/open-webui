# Managing Chat History

LexLuma automatically saves every conversation you start, making it easy to revisit prior research, extract arguments, or continue earlier analyses. This section outlines how to manage your chat history effectively so you can maintain clarity and order across multiple legal matters.

---

## 📁 Viewing Past Chats

Your complete chat history appears in the **sidebar** under the Chats Section. Each entry includes:

- Chat title  
- Date and time  
- Workspace (if organized)  
- Quick-access menu  

Scrolling the list allows you to instantly return to earlier research.

---

## ✏️ Renaming Chats for Better Organization

Click the **options menu** beside a chat and choose **Rename**.

For legal workflows, consider naming patterns such as:

- *“Judicial Review – Grounds”*  
- *“Land Act – Adverse Possession Summary”*  
- *“Criminal Appeal – Evidence Evaluation”*  

Clear names save time and reduce confusion.

---

## 🗑️ Deleting, Archiving, or Restoring Chats

You can manage clutter by:

- **Archiving** chats not actively in use  
- **Deleting** chats that are no longer needed  
- **Restoring** archived chats when needed  

Archived chats are stored separately but remain searchable.

---

## 🔄 Continuing Research Across Sessions

LexLuma preserves:

- Your uploaded documents  
- The entire conversation flow  
- AI responses and citations  

You can pick up research anytime—even months later—without losing context.
