# Models Quick Access Panel

LexLuma includes a **Models Quick Access Panel** in the sidebar, giving you immediate access to the specialized AI models available within the platform. This feature allows you to switch or select models suited for different legal tasks seamlessly.

---

## 🤖 Why the Panel Matters

Different AI models have different strengths—some excel at summarization, others at statutory interpretation or case law comparison. The Quick Access Panel ensures you can choose the right tool without leaving your research flow.

---

## 🔧 Key Features

### **1. Model Selection**
You can choose between LexLuma’s optimized legal LLMs for tasks such as:

- Summarizing long judgments  
- Drafting legal arguments  
- Comparing statutory provisions  
- Conducting in-depth research queries  

### **2. Model Descriptions**
Hovering over a model shows:

- Use case  
- Strengths  
- Speed vs depth profile  

### **3. Fast Switching**
Switch models mid-chat when needed. LexLuma will adapt the upcoming responses accordingly.

---

## 📘 When to Change Models?

You may switch models when:

- You need a more analytical response  
- You’re drafting structured arguments  
- You're summarizing large documents  
- You want faster general reasoning  

LexLuma ensures each query still draws from the same verified legal knowledge base.
