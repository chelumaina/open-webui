# Pinning and Organizing Chats

Organizing your research efficiently helps maintain clarity and momentum—especially when working across multiple legal matters. LexLuma provides powerful tools to prioritize, categorize, and structure all your conversations.

---

## 📌 Pinning Critical Chats

Pinning is ideal for chats you access frequently.

Pinned chats always appear at the top of the sidebar, ensuring fast access to:

- Ongoing cases  
- Urgent legal research  
- Matters requiring repeated follow-up  

Common examples include:
- *“Criminal Appeal – Hearing Next Week”*  
- *“Contract Drafting – Urgent Revisions”*

Pins can be added or removed through the chat options menu.

---

## 🗂️ Organizing Chats into Workspaces

Workspaces function like digital case folders. You can move chats into:

- Matter-specific workspaces  
- Practice areas (e.g., Commercial Law, Criminal Law)  
- Teams or departments  

This makes long-term research management significantly easier.

---

## 🚌 Moving, Renaming, and Sorting

LexLuma allows you to:

- **Rename** chats for clarity  
- **Move** chats between workspaces  
- **Sort** chats by recency, alphabetically, or by workspace  

These tools help legal practitioners keep complex research environments clean and navigable.

---

## 🧽 Archiving for Cleanliness

Chats you no longer need daily can be archived. They remain accessible but hidden from your main list, helping you focus on current tasks.
