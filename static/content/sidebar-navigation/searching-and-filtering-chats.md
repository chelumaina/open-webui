# Searching and Filtering Chats

As your legal research grows, so does your list of chats. LexLuma’s search and filtering tools help you quickly locate specific matters, issues, or case files without manually scrolling through long lists.

---

## 🔎 Sidebar Search Bar

At the top of the Chats Section, you’ll find a **search bar** that lets you instantly locate chats by:

- Chat title  
- Keywords used in the conversation  
- Workspace name  
- Document names uploaded  

Search results update in real-time, making navigation seamless.

---

## 🧪 Filtering Tools

Filters help you narrow down chats based on:

- **Workspace / Folder**  
- **Date created**  
- **Pinned chats**  
- **Archived chats**  
- **Recent activity**  

These filters are especially helpful during fast-paced litigation or legislative drafting.

---

## 📌 Use Cases for Legal Professionals

### **Case Management**
Quickly find conversations related to:
- Specific clients  
- Case numbers  
- Themes such as jurisdiction, evidence, or sentencing  

### **Document-Based Search**
Locate chats where you analyzed:
- A particular judgment  
- Legislation excerpt  
- Gazette notice  

### **Team or Organizational Use**
If your organization uses shared workspaces, you can filter by:

- Team folders  
- Matter tags  
- Project categories
