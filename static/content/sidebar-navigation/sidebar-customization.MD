# Sidebar Customization

LexLuma allows you to personalize the sidebar to match your workflow preferences. Whether you prefer a minimalist layout or a detailed research dashboard, customization options help maximize efficiency and comfort.

---

## 🎛️ Customizable Elements

### **1. Collapse / Expand Sidebar**
Toggle between:

- Full view with workspaces, chats, and tools  
- Compact mode showing only icons  

Great for small screens or distraction-free work.

### **2. Reorder Workspaces**
Drag and drop workspaces to place priority cases at the top.

### **3. Toggle Panels**
Show or hide:

- Models Quick Access Panel  
- Workspaces section  
- Recent chats  
- Pinned chats  

This ensures the sidebar displays only what you need.

---

## 👓 Display Preferences

You can adjust sidebar appearance:

- Light or dark theme  
- Compact list spacing  
- Large or small chat tiles  

These settings enhance readability for extended research sessions.

---

## 🧑‍⚖️ Why Customization Helps Legal Workflows

Legal practitioners manage:

- Multiple clients  
- Rapid deadlines  
- Heavy documentation  

Customizing the sidebar ensures your most important tools remain front-and-center, improving response time and research efficiency.
