# Workspaces Overview

Workspaces in LexLuma help you structure research related to cases, clients, departments, or legal themes. They function like organized folders, allowing you to keep related chats, documents, and research in one place.

---

## 📁 Why Workspaces Matter

Legal practice often involves handling:

- Multiple cases  
- Draft legislation  
- Appeals and reviews  
- Research across different legal disciplines  

Instead of letting everything mix together, Workspaces let you compartmentalize matters just like physical case files.

---

## 🧱 Workspace Structure

A workspace can include:

- Chats  
- Uploaded documents  
- Drafts or summaries  
- Categorized notes  

Each workspace provides a streamlined environment for focused legal analysis.

---

## 🧭 How to Access Workspaces

In the sidebar, workspaces appear as expandable folders. Clicking one reveals all related chats and files. This structure enhances:

- Clarity  
- Navigation  
- Team collaboration (if enabled)  

---

## 📌 Example Workspace Uses

- **Case-Based Workspace:**

- **Practice Area Workspace:**
