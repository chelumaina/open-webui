# Accessing Model Information

LexLuma allows users to access detailed **model information** to understand capabilities, limitations, and ideal use cases. This ensures informed model selection for legal research tasks.

---

## 📂 How to Access Model Details

1. Open the **Models Panel** in the chat interface.  
2. Hover over a model name to see a tooltip with a brief description.  
3. Click **More Info** for a detailed overview:
   - Supported tasks  
   - Input and output types  
   - Context window size  
   - Recommended usage scenarios  
   - Known limitations

---

## 🧠 Why Access Model Information?

- **Accuracy:** Ensures the model fits the complexity of your legal research.  
- **Efficiency:** Selects the fastest and most effective model for the task.  
- **Contextual Relevance:** Ensures the AI considers the appropriate legal materials.  
- **Compliance:** Understand the model’s RAG sources to validate citations.

---

## 🔧 Best Practices

- Review model details before starting **critical legal research**.  
- Check **context window limits** to prevent truncated analysis on long documents.  
- Keep a reference sheet of models most suitable for different legal domains (criminal, corporate, constitutional).  
- Regularly monitor updates, as LexLuma models are continually improved.
