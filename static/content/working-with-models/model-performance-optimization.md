# Model Performance Optimization

LexLuma offers several tools and strategies to **optimize model performance** for legal research tasks. Efficient model use ensures faster, more accurate, and contextually relevant responses.

---

## 🛠️ Key Optimization Strategies

### **1. Choose the Right Model**
- Text-only for pure legal research  
- Multimodal for scanned or complex documents  
- Specialized models for coding or creative drafting

### **2. Adjust Model Parameters**
- **Temperature:** Controls creativity  
- **Response Length:** Determines detail  
- **Context Window:** Ensures relevant historical context

### **3. Manage Workspaces and Chats**
- Keep related chats together  
- Archive completed tasks  
- Pin high-priority conversations

### **4. Use Regeneration and Editing**
- Refine responses for accuracy  
- Compare multiple outputs for consistency

---

## 🔧 Best Practices for Legal Workflows

- Regularly check **model info and capabilities**.  
- Split large tasks across multiple chats or models.  
- Use **workspace organization** to reduce cognitive load.  
- Cross-check AI-generated insights against primary legal sources.

---

## 📘 Summary

By combining **appropriate model selection**, **parameter tuning**, and **workspace management**, LexLuma users can maximize AI efficiency, accuracy, and reliability for all types of legal research and drafting tasks.
