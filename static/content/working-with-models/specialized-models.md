# Specialized Models (Coding, Creative, etc.)

LexLuma provides **specialized AI models** designed to handle unique legal tasks that go beyond text-only analysis. These models are tailored for coding, creative drafting, or analytical tasks, enabling legal professionals to streamline workflows and improve productivity.

---

## 🛠️ Types of Specialized Models

### **1. Coding Models**
- Automate repetitive legal drafting tasks, such as contracts or compliance templates.  
- Generate scripts to extract case details or process large datasets.  
- Integrate with document management systems for automated workflows.

### **2. Creative Models**
- Draft persuasive arguments, policy memos, or legal letters.  
- Provide alternative phrasings or stylistic variations in briefs.  
- Support scenario planning and legislative simulations.

### **3. Analytical Models**
- Conduct multi-step reasoning for complex case analyses.  
- Identify patterns or discrepancies across rulings, statutes, or exhibits.  
- Generate risk assessments or predictive insights for legal strategies.

---

## 🔍 When to Use Specialized Models

- Automating contract or legal document drafting.  
- Producing creative briefs or legal correspondence.  
- Performing comparative analysis across multiple legal sources.  
- Handling repetitive tasks such as case metadata extraction.

---

## 💡 Best Practices

- Define the task clearly to select the appropriate specialized model.  
- Combine specialized models with **text-only** or **vision models** for hybrid research workflows.  
- Use iterative prompting to refine output, leveraging **message actions** like regeneration or editing.  
- Monitor model suggestions for legal accuracy and cross-check against source documents.
