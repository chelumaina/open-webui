# Switching Models Mid-Conversation

LexLuma allows seamless **model switching during a conversation**, enabling legal professionals to adapt to changing research needs without losing context. This flexibility is crucial for multi-step legal analysis.

---

## 🔄 How to Switch Models

1. Open the **Models Panel** while in a conversation.  
2. Select a new model suited to your updated task.  
3. LexLuma preserves the **existing chat context**, ensuring continuity in responses.  
4. The AI will generate new answers based on the selected model while maintaining prior conversation history.

---

## 🧩 When to Switch Models

- **Complex Tasks:** Switch from text-only to multimodal if you need document image analysis.  
- **Drafting Variations:** Move to a creative model for alternative arguments or briefs.  
- **Automation:** Switch to a coding model for generating scripts or templates.  
- **Performance Optimization:** Use lighter models for faster responses on simple queries.

---

## 💡 Best Practices

- Confirm the new model’s **context window** supports the conversation length.  
- Combine **regeneration** with model switching to refine outputs.  
- Keep the chat organized using **workspace filters** to track which model generated which responses.  
- Always cross-check critical legal outputs after switching models to ensure accuracy.
