# Temperature and Creativity Controls

LexLuma’s **temperature setting** allows you to control the creativity and randomness of AI responses. This parameter is particularly important when drafting legal content, generating alternative arguments, or exploring multiple interpretations of legislation and case law.

---

## 🌡️ Understanding Temperature

- **Low Temperature (0–0.3):**  
  Produces precise, deterministic responses. Ideal for:
  - Statutory interpretation  
  - Case law summarization  
  - Technical drafting of contracts or pleadings

- **Medium Temperature (0.4–0.7):**  
  Balances creativity and accuracy. Useful for:
  - Argument generation  
  - Legal opinion drafting  
  - Policy scenario simulations

- **High Temperature (0.8–1.0):**  
  Generates creative, exploratory responses. Best for:
  - Brainstorming arguments  
  - Drafting persuasive or policy documents  
  - Exploring multiple interpretations

---

## 🔧 How to Adjust Temperature

1. Open the **Model Settings Panel**.  
2. Locate the **Temperature Slider**.  
3. Adjust according to task complexity and desired creativity.  
4. Save changes to apply them immediately to the current chat session.

---

## 💡 Best Practices

- Use **low temperature** for precise legal citations or factual summaries.  
- Use **medium temperature** when generating multiple drafts or perspectives.  
- Use **high temperature** cautiously; verify outputs for legal accuracy.  
- Combine with **response length settings** for detailed analysis.
