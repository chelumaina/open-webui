# Text-Only Models

Text-only models are the foundation of LexLuma’s AI suite, optimized for tasks that involve **reading, summarizing, drafting, or interpreting legal text**. They are ideal for lawyers, clerks, and researchers working with statutes, rulings, or legal memos.

---

## 🧾 Capabilities

- **Legal Summaries:** Extract key points from court decisions or legislative documents.  
- **Issue Identification:** Highlight potential legal issues or inconsistencies.  
- **Drafting Assistance:** Generate memos, briefs, or contracts based on context.  
- **Comparative Analysis:** Compare sections across multiple statutes or rulings.  
- **Citation Linking:** RAG-powered references ensure transparency and accuracy.

---

## 🔧 When to Use Text-Only Models

- Handling **purely textual documents** without images or scans.  
- Tasks requiring **high accuracy** in legal reasoning.  
- Quick **fact extraction**, summarization, or legal memo drafting.  
- Iterative questioning or context-aware discussions over multiple steps.

---

## 💡 Best Practices

- Provide **concise and clear prompts** to improve output quality.  
- Attach supporting documents for more detailed analysis.  
- Combine with **message actions** for regenerating or editing responses.  
- Use **workspace organization** to manage multiple research threads efficiently.
