# Understanding Different Model Types

LexLuma provides several types of AI models to address the diverse needs of legal professionals. Knowing the differences between them is crucial for efficient and accurate research.

---

## 🧩 Main Model Categories

### **1. Text-Only Models**
Specialized in legal reasoning, drafting, and text analysis. Ideal for:
- Case summarization  
- Statutory interpretation  
- Legal memo drafting  
- Issue extraction

### **2. Vision and Multimodal Models**
Combine text and image understanding. Useful for:
- OCR-processed statutes and gazettes  
- Annotated court decisions  
- Evidence review and document analysis  

### **3. Specialized Models**
Tailored for specific tasks:
- **Coding Models:** Automate legal workflows, contract generation, and data processing scripts.  
- **Creative Models:** Draft persuasive arguments, legal letters, or policy scenarios.  
- **Analytical Models:** Support scenario planning, risk analysis, or legislative comparisons.

---

## 🛠️ Choosing the Right Model

- **Start with text-only models** for general research.  
- **Use multimodal models** when working with scanned or complex visual documents.  
- **Select specialized models** for coding, drafting, or simulations.  

Always consider:
- Task complexity  
- Document type  
- Need for creativity versus accuracy
