# Vision and Multimodal Models

LexLuma’s **Vision and Multimodal Models** extend text-only capabilities by understanding both **text and images**. These models are ideal for legal documents that include scans, tables, or annotated exhibits.

---

## 🖼️ Key Features

- **OCR Processing:** Convert scanned PDFs or images into searchable text.  
- **Document Interpretation:** Understand charts, tables, and legal diagrams.  
- **Cross-modal Analysis:** Combine visual and textual information for comprehensive insights.  
- **Enhanced Summaries:** Include references to visual elements in outputs.

---

## 🔍 When to Use Multimodal Models

- Working with **scanned gazettes, rulings, or exhibits**.  
- Analyzing **complex documents** with both tables and text.  
- Extracting **legal information from images or annotated PDFs**.  
- Reviewing visual evidence alongside legal commentary.

---

## 💡 Best Practices

- Ensure scanned documents are **high-resolution and clear**.  
- Provide context about the document type in your prompt.  
- Combine with text-only models for textual deep dives.  
- Use the **message actions** to regenerate or refine extracted insights.
